Cricket Tournament 2011

With a long cherished, long dreamed World Cup victory this April, Indian cricket was in everybody's mind for a whole fortnight. This gave more enthusiasm and brought all the Indian students at UMass on to the field.

The cricket tournaments conducted by ISA have involved huge participation every year and this year was no different. There were two tournaments conducted during Summer 2011. Creating teams is an exercise in itself, contributing in rapport building and a mix of various cultures of India. Thus, the tournaments were held not just as a fun-filled summer sport but also as an event to bring together students of Fall and Spring semesters.

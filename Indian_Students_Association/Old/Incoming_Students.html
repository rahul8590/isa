Incoming Students - Please read this carefully.

 

If you are an incoming student for Fall/Spring 2011/2012, this is what you will have to do for ISA to help you....


Every incoming student, please join the UMASS ISA Mailing list. You can go to the mailing list by clicking here.


  1. 1.Join the umass_inbound group on Yahoo Groups.

  2. 2.Fill out your arrival information by clicking here.

  3. 3.Start looking for accommodation. we have FAQ pages that have a lot of information regarding this. please pull them up and have a look.


Things that have been planned for the incoming  Fall 2011 batch:

  1. 1.Airport pickups to be arranged from Hartford and Boston. Please note that we will not be able to pick you up from New York.

  2. 2.Temporary accommodation arrangements till you find your own apartments.

    (vacancies will be posted on the classifieds page so that you can contact the posters and work out things)

  1. 3.Mentoring : Each incoming student will be assigned a mentor from his or her respective department. this person will be your single stop for all queries right from “Should i bring a cooker or not?” to “Whom can i go to for funding?”
